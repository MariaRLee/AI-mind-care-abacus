算盤故事與玩法｜Abacus History & Play Guide

一、算盤小故事｜A Short History

算盤是流傳已久的東方計算工具，透過位值、算珠與手指操作， 將抽象數字轉化為看得見、可以動手操作的計算過程。

The abacus is a traditional calculating tool that turns abstract numbers into visible, hands-on bead movements based on place value.

二、如何看算盤｜How to Read the Abacus

  • 上珠＝5｜Upper bead = 5
  • 每顆下珠＝1｜Each lower bead = 1
  • 靠近橫樑才計數｜Count beads near the beam
  • 8＝上珠 5＋下珠 3｜8 = 5 + 3

每一直行代表一個位值；由右向左依序為個、十、百、千…… 小數點右方則為十分位與百分位。

Each vertical rod represents one place value. Moving left gives ones, tens, hundreds, thousands, and more; the two rods on the right represent tenths and hundredths.

三、如何玩｜How to Play

  1. 選擇難度、小數位與題數。Select difficulty, decimals, and number of questions.
  2. 按下「開始 Start」。Press Start.
  3. 第一個數字已預先放在算盤上。The first number is preloaded.
  4. 依照加法或減法撥動算珠。Move the beads to add or subtract.
  5. 按「提交 Submit」查看結果。Press Submit to check your answer.

不需要背誦傳統算盤口訣，也可以依照數字慢慢撥珠練習。
No traditional abacus formula is required—move the beads at your own pace.
